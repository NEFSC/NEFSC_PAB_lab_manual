    Striped dolphin (Stenella coeruleoalba)

    General information:

    • Gregarious with pods varying in size from a few to over 1000 individuals, avg school size of 100-500 individuals (Papale et al, 2012)
    • Historically difficult to classify (most difficult in Oswald et al, 2003)1
    • Can resemble common dolphin, spotted dolphin, and bottlenose dolphin
    • Two categories of vocalization: tonal whistles, pulsed sounds (which are normally characterized by short, broadband clicks)

    Whistles:

    • Frequency
      • Azores, max fq range 7.6-30.7 kHz, mean 17.1 kHz, strd dev 3.5 kHz (Papale et al, 2012)
      • Azores, min fq range 1.1-13.5 kHz, mean 7.8 kHz, std dev 1.7 kHz (Papale et al, 2012)
      • Azores, fq range 598 Hz – 23.6 kHz, mean 9.2 kHz, std dev 3.5 kHz (Papale et al, 2012)
    • Duration
      • Azores, range 0.15-2.23, mean 0.95, std dev 0.33 (Papale et al, 2012)
    • Waveform
      • Avg of 1 inflection point, but can be up to 11 (Papale et al, 2012)
